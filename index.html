Qiyam

Read the Quran every night.

Qiyam helps you build and keep a nightly habit of standing with the Quran. Set a reading plan, follow it page by page in a beautiful authentic Mushaf, and watch your streak grow — from the first ayah to a complete Khatmah.

What's inside

  • Nightly reading plans — Khatmah in the nights you choose, 100 ayat a night, or a custom pace.
  • Authentic Mushaf — Uthmanic script (QCF), page by page, all 604 pages, with optional Tajweed coloring.
  • Prayer times & Qibla — accurate times with a live Fajr countdown and a built-in compass.
  • Listen & learn — ayah-by-ayah recitation, word-by-word, and inline tafsir.
  • Track progress — surah checklist, Khatmah history, day streaks, and tagged bookmarks.
  • Revision (Muraja'a) — plan and track what you've memorized.
  • Works offline — download reciters and reading packs for use anywhere.
