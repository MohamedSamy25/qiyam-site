Frequently asked

How do prayer times work?

Enable location access and Qiyam calculates accurate prayer times and the Qibla direction on your device. You can change access in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. The Mushaf is available offline, and you can download reciters and reading packs from within the app to read and listen without a connection.

How do I set up a reading plan?

Open the Qiyam tab and choose a plan — complete the Quran (Khatmah) in a number of nights you pick, read a fixed amount each night, or set a custom pace. You can reschedule anytime.

My progress or streak looks wrong.

Your data is stored on your device. If something looks off, email us with a short description and we'll help you sort it out.
